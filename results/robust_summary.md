## Interpretation

- Mean service gain over graph FedAvg safe: 1.78 percentage points.
- Mean service gain over local FedAvg safe: 1.98 percentage points.
- Mean service gain over no-guard ablation: 2.06 percentage points.
- Mean graph-feature service gain over local-feature FedAvg: 0.20 percentage points.
- Mean charging-cost change vs graph FedAvg safe: -0.99%.
- Mean charging-cost change vs EDF-safe: -8.22%.
- The strongest evidence is feasibility plus communication efficiency: the
  proposed policy keeps zero voltage violations across all synthetic scenarios
  while using far fewer training bytes than full graph FedAvg.
- The superiority claim should remain multi-objective. Cost is close to graph
  FedAvg safe, not uniformly lower in every scenario.
